Zodiac App

Using basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I built a fun landing page on a topic of my choosing: the Chinese Zodiac. I provided a brief history of its origins and created a fun button! The button is a Zodiac Animal Generator which works by the user entering their birthday and then utilizing Javascript arrays, if/else statements, and functions it will retrieve a Zodiac Animal based on the year. This project was a great way to learn something new while getting to explore a topic I already loved.

2022 is the Year of the Tiger - the king of all beasts and the center of attention in most social circles! This year symbolizes strength and bravery. Babies born this year will also be smart, confident, humorous, and good at socializing.

Weather App

I created a Weather App with a Search Engine feature and a forecast for the week ahead. To create this I used HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap and API integration. The project really pushed the limits as we only used Vanilla Javascript - but were able to make something pretty complex. The three quick links are the home cities of my family. I'm based in Charlotte, my parents are in Houston, and my sister is in Portland.

Always Sunny is a reference to one of my favorite TV Shows, but also to the weather that I'm always hoping for every time I check the app. The more sunshine the better!

Interior Design

Before I shifted my interests to Front-End Development, I come from a background of over 10 years in Customer Service and Management, and over 6 years in Interior Design. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Fashion Merchandising and while I started in apparel, I eventually moved into home furnishings. My career has spanned retailers and design groups like Anthropologie, Pottery Barn, and Modsy.

Interior Design is a huge passion of mine and I love helping my clients bring their visions to life. I've been lucky enough to work on everything from townhomes, to Air BnB properties (on multiple continents), to corporate offices. I particularly enjoy designing modern, multi-purpose spaces and pushing the boundaries of style and function.

For a complete work history check out my LinkedIn or my resume.
