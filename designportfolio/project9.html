Transitional Owner's Suite

Project: This client was looking to make her master bedroom into her favorite room in the house. She wanted a stylish sitting area and a calming bedrrom. For colors she was looking for bold blue tones, mixed with with her favorites of gray and chrome. She described her style as "I like things that sparkle" - so I definitely brought some glam into the design. I created a transitional space with a mixture of modern, traditional and luxurious elements.

Budget: $10,000-15,000
