Colorful Playroom

Project: A growing family was relocating from NYC to the suburbs and had just bought a beautiful mid-century ranch home. What was originally intended to be a dining room (later converted to an office by the previous owners) was set to become a playroom for 3 kids under age 5. The client wanted an activity table, a reading corner, a comfy chair for an adult to sit, and to make good use of the existing built-ins. Through Modsy, I created two fun play spaces that checked all the boxes.

Budget: $2,000-$3,500
